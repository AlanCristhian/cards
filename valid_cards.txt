Heal: Append a Health Chip on Player Chip Stack.
Postpone: Append a Turn on Player.
Harm: Remove a Health Chip on Opponent Chip Stack.
Absorb: Move a Health Chip from Opponent Chip Stack to Player Chip Stack.
Accumulate: Move a Card from Player Life Stack to Player Hand.
Resurrect: Move a Card from Player Death Stack to Player Life Stack.
Steal: Move a Card from Opponent Life Stack to Player Life Stack.
Crop: Move a Card from Opponent Hand to Opponent Life Stack.
Kill: Move a Card from Opponent Life Stack to Opponent Death Stack.
